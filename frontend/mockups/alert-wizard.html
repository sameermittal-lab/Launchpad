What happens next:
  • Indeed sends a daily digest around 7am local time
  • LinkedIn sends the first alert within 24 hours of saving
  • Each digest usually contains 5-20 roles after LinkedIn's filter, 10-40 from Indeed
  • LaunchPad auto-extracts each listing and evaluates the top matches
  • You'll see them on your Pipeline board under "Evaluated"
Heads-up: alerts expire if you don't engage with the emails. If you start ignoring them, Indeed/LinkedIn will stop sending. Come back to this wizard once a month to refresh or tune criteria.
